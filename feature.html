Features That Keep You Moving

VStep transforms your workouts into gaming actions. Below are the core features that make it fun and powerful.

1. Motion-Based Control

Your steps and jumps are captured via mobile sensors and transmitted to your PC in real time.

2. Bluetooth & Wi-Fi Sync

Connect wirelessly to your computer for smooth gameplay without cables or delays.

3. Custom Game Mapping

Map different movements to in-game actions. Jump = spacebar, Squat = crouch — full control.

4. Calorie Tracker

Track calories burned while playing. Compete with friends for fitness goals!
