Support & Troubleshooting

Need help getting started or facing connection issues? We’re here to assist.

Common Issues

  • 🔌 Device Not Connecting: Ensure both phone and PC are on the same Wi-Fi network.
  • 📱 Motion Not Detected: Grant motion and sensor permissions in the app settings.
  • ⚙️ Game Not Responding: Restart the VStep PC client and try reconnecting.
